Send the pictures — this page grows all camp.

Photos from the studios, the client meetings, the crash tests and the showcase all belong here. Facilitators: drop them in the camp Drive and they get added to this page in a batch — no design work needed on your side.

Before a photo goes up

Campers are minors. Every image on this page must be one the camp is cleared to publish: no full names beside faces, nothing that identifies a school or address, nothing a parent hasn't consented to through the camp's media permission. When in doubt, it stays off the public page and lives in Classroom instead — where only the class can see it. Group shots and over-the-shoulder build photos are the safest and, honestly, the best-looking.
